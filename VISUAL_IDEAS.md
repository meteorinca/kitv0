## Art Direction Notes

**Style:** Warm cream/parchment background (#f5f0e8). Pencil sketch lines with loose, confident strokes. Watercolor washes in teal, amber, muted red, and dusty green. No clean vector look — imperfect is intentional. Think: naturalist field journal meets vintage science textbook.

**Typography in illustrations:** Mix of handwritten labels (for annotations) and printed serif text (for titles). Size annotations should use small caps with ruler lines.

**Color palette for washes:**
- Teal: `#22d1c3` (Scale 1, data flow)
- Amber: `#f5c842` (Scale 0, sparks, electricity)
- Muted sage: `#7aab8a` (Scale 2, logic paths)
- Dusty violet: `#a78bfa` (Scale 3, loops)
- Burnt orange: `#f5a442` (Scale 4, sensors)
- Coral red: `#f87171` (Scale 5, energy, rockets)

**Size comparison format (like Size of Life):**
Each illustration should have a small measurement annotation: a thin vertical or horizontal rule with a label at one end. Examples: "5nm wide", "3 inches long", "2,048 cores".
